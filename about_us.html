About Us

Providing comprehensive rehabilitation programs tailored to individual needs.

Who Will Benefit from Our Services?

  • ✔ Neurological conditions: Stroke, MS, GBS, spinal cord injury, Parkinson’s, Traumatic Brain Injury, Dementia, Alzheimer’s, Vertigo, etc.
  • ✔ Orthopedic conditions: Post-surgery recovery, arthritis, frozen shoulder, fractures, sports injuries, chronic pain.
  • ✔ Pediatric care: Developmental delays, Cerebral Palsy, Autism, Down’s Syndrome, ADHD, Spina Bifida.
  • ✔ Aged population: Reduced mobility, balance issues, frequent falls, cognitive decline.
  • ✔ Other conditions requiring structured inpatient rehabilitation.

How Are We Different?

Faster Recovery Through Admission Facility

Patients requiring intensive rehabilitation can benefit from multiple daily sessions, improving various domains at once for faster recovery.

Multispeciality Collaborative Approach

Our team of experts—including physiotherapists, occupational therapists, speech therapists, neurologists, and psychologists—works together to provide a **unified care plan**.

Making Caregiving Easy

We guide family members and caregivers on how to handle and support patients, easing their physical burden while improving emotional availability.

All Services Under One Roof

From **physiotherapy to diagnostics like X-rays, blood tests, ECGs**, and neurogenetic testing, we provide a one-stop solution for rehabilitation.

Specialized Rehabilitation Programs

We ensure engaging and innovative treatment strategies that keep our patients motivated throughout their rehabilitation journey.

Discharge Protocol for a Smooth Transition

Our goal is to help patients **recover faster, regain independence, and return to normal life as soon as possible**.
