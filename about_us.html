Our Journey, Vision, and Mission

Providing comprehensive rehabilitation programs tailored to individual needs

Who will benefits from our services:

  • ✔ With Neurological conditions requiring intensive rehabilitation (Stroke, MS, GBS, spinal cord injury, Parkinson’s disease, traumatic brain injury, Dementia, Alzheimer's, Vertigo etc).
  • ✔ With orthopedic conditions (Post hip & knee replacement, spine surgery, ligament reconstruction, Arthritis, Frozen shoulder, Spondylitis, Foot Deformities, Fractures, Heel/ Neck/ Knee/ Back/ Shoulder Pain, Sports Injuries, Scoliosis, Tingling & Numbness).
  • ✔ Pediatric Population (With Developmental delays, Cerebral Palsy, Learning disorders, Autism, Down's Syndrome, Hyperactivity Disorders, Attention Deficit disorders, Spina Bifida)
  • ✔ Aged population (With reduced mobility, frequent falls, aches & pains, balance issues, Urinary Incontinence, Cognitive Decline)
  • ✔ Any other condition requiring structured inpatient rehabilitation as determined by the consulting physiotherapist or referring physician.
