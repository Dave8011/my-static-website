Our Key Programs


Balance & Postural Control

Our Balance & Postural Control program is designed to prevent falls and improve stability for patients with neurological or orthopedic conditions. We utilize advanced interactive motion sensors and computerized posturography to assess and treat balance deficits.

Conditions Treated

  • Parkinson’s Disease
  • Ataxia
  • Stroke recovery
  • Geriatric balance issues

Our Approach

We combine traditional physiotherapy drills with state-of-the-art technology to create a safe and effective training environment. Patients engage in fun, interactive games that challenge their stability, reaction time, and coordination.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Vestibular Rehabilitation Therapy (VRT) is a specialized form of therapy intended to alleviate both the primary and secondary problems caused by vestibular disorders.

Why It's Needed

Vertigo, dizziness, and imbalance can severely impact quality of life. Our specialized program targets the root cause of these symptoms.

Therapy Techniques

  • Gaze Stabilization Exercises
  • Canalith Repositioning Maneuvers (Epley)
  • Habituation Exercises

Visual Therapy

Visual Therapy is crucial for patients suffering from vision deficits due to neurological conditions such as stroke or traumatic brain injury.

Focus Areas

We work on improving visual tracking, convergence, and visual-spatial processing to help patients navigate their environment safely.

Benefits

  • Improved reading ability
  • Better depth perception
  • Reduced visual fatigue

Spasticity & Posture Management

Effective management of spasticity and posture is vital for preventing contractures and improving independence in daily activities.

Comprehensive Care

Our approach includes stretching protocols, splinting, and positioning strategies to combat the effects of a sedentary lifestyle and muscle stiffness.

Technologies Used

  • Electrical Stimulation
  • Biofeedback
  • Custom Orthotics consultation

Cognition-Enhancing Activities

Our cognitive rehabilitation program focuses on restoring memory, attention, and executive functions impaired by brain injury or degeneration.

Training Modules

We utilize a mix of traditional pen-and-paper tasks, board games, and digital screen-based activities to simulate real-world challenges.

Key Outcomes

  • Enhanced Memory and Recall
  • Improved Attention Span
  • Faster Reaction Times
  • Better Problem-Solving Skills

Pain Management

Chronic pain requires a multi-faceted approach. We offer evidence-based treatments to alleviate pain and restore function.

Techniques

  • Dry Needling & Cupping Therapy
  • Manual Therapy & Manipulations
  • Kinesiotaping
  • Advanced Electrotherapy (IFT, TENS, Ultrasound)

Conditions

Back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, and post-surgical pain.
