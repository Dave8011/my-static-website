Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral?

While a referral is helpful, it is not mandatory. You can book an appointment directly with our specialists.

Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

Yes, The Rehab House is fully wheelchair accessible with elevator access and wide corridors.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Please wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement during therapy sessions.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes, we offer home visits for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic. Please contact us for details.
