Understanding Vestibular Therapy

November 02, 2025

Feeling dizzy or off-balance can be debilitating. Vestibular Rehabilitation Therapy (VRT) is a specialized form of therapy intended to alleviate both the primary and secondary problems caused by vestibular disorders.

What is the Vestibular System?

It includes parts of the inner ear and brain that process the sensory information involved with controlling balance and eye movements.

How VRT Helps

VRT uses an exercise-based program primarily designed to reduce vertigo and dizziness, gaze instability, and/or imbalance and falls.
