Meet Dr. Sakshi Palsambkar: Occupational Therapist (Gold Medalist)


Dr. Sakshi Palsambkar is a distinguished Occupational Therapist and a Gold Medalist in her field. She specializes in both Adult and Pediatric Neuro-Rehabilitation, bringing a wealth of knowledge and skill to her practice.


Her Expertise Include:

  • Cognitive Perceptual Therapy
  • Behavioural Interventions
  • Certified Ergonomic Therapist

Dr. Sakshi is committed to enhancing the quality of life for her patients through targeted therapies. Whether working with children or adults, she employs advanced techniques in neuro-rehabilitation to achieve the best possible outcomes.
